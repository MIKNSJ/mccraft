ABOUT

Our business has been around for at least 15 years serving at many locations across the globe. We want to thank our founder, Steve for supporting his employeers and providing the joy of a delicious meal to our customers. Steve started off with nothing and slept through nights without a shelter living in the overworld. Eventually Steve, known after for conquering the ender dragon, would start a farm with his fellow villager friends to start a local bakery. This bakery would be the start of a food business where more types of food would emerge in the upcoming years.
